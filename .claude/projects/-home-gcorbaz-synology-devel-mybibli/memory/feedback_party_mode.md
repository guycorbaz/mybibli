Guy systematically uses Party Mode (P) at every step of the PRD workflow to get multi-agent perspectives (John/PM, Winston/Architect, Sally/UX, Mary/Analyst) before validating decisions. He also asks "avez-vous d'autres points à traiter?" to ensure thorough coverage.
**Why:** He values thorough upfront analysis and wants multiple perspectives to catch blind spots before they become development problems.
**How to apply:** During planning phases (UX, architecture, epics), proactively offer Party Mode and encourage thorough exploration. During implementation, the foundation should be solid enough to move faster.
